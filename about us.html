Teen Titans

GameLife is a website for make it easy for users to access and play mobile games and to keep up to date with the latest flash games and PC games news. Our group is called "Teen Titans" we run a website project called Gamelife. Our main goal of creating this website is to make it easy for users to play their favorite mobile games by pressing the on-screen button without having to download it for flash games and PC games. We also provide a platform through the community blog to allow users to interact with other players to share experiences and also exchange ideas for play patterns that make them stand out in every stage of the game. Besides, we also provide updates on mobile games that are of interest to us as well as new flash games and PC games that will be released shortly. We also provided a user-friendly game recommendation. We are gaming enthusiasts who are excited about this platform to share the fun of playing mobile games with others.
